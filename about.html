About South Atlantic LNG West Africa

South Atlantic LNG West Africa Limited (SA LNG) is a Nigerian energy infrastructure company.

Who We Are

South Atlantic LNG West Africa Limited is focused on developing small-scale LNG infrastructure that supports Nigeria’s gas utilization agenda through virtual pipelines and gas-to-power solutions.

Our projects are designed to monetize stranded and flared gas, delivering clean, efficient fuel to power plants and industrial users who rely on expensive and carbon-intensive diesel and heavy fuel oil.

Our Vision

To be a leading virtual pipeline LNG platform in West Africa, driving gas-to-power adoption and eliminating routine gas flaring.

Our Mission

  • Deliver reliable virtual pipeline gas solutions
  • Enable gas-to-power for industrial and embedded generation
  • Reduce routine gas flaring through gas monetisation
  • Improve energy security and affordability
  • Support Nigeria’s Decade of Gas initiative

Why Virtual Pipeline LNG

  • Brings gas to customers where pipelines do not exist
  • Faster deployment than conventional gas infrastructure
  • Lower upfront capital requirements
  • Ideal for gas-to-power projects and industrial clusters
  • Enables rapid market entry and scalable growth

Gas Flaring Reduction & Sustainability

Routine gas flaring represents both an environmental challenge and a lost economic opportunity.

Our Approach:

  • Captures flared and stranded gas
  • Converts gas into LNG for productive use
  • Reduces greenhouse gas emissions
  • Supports Nigeria’s flaring reduction targets
  • Promotes cleaner energy transition
