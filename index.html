About Us

South Atlantic LNG West Africa Limited (SA LNG) is a Nigerian energy infrastructure company delivering small-scale LNG and virtual pipeline solutions that unlock natural gas for gas-to-power, industrial, and commercial applications across Nigeria and West Africa.

By converting natural gas into LNG, we enable reliable energy supply to customers without pipeline access, while supporting gas flaring reduction, lower emissions, and sustainable economic growth.
